Questions? We can help.

Do you offer any special pricing?
Yes we do! We offer discounts for nonprofits, educational institutions, and classroom use. We also offer a larger yearly discount for 50+ person teams. You can reach out to us at sales@harvestapp.com for more information.

How do I sign up for the free plan?
You can upgrade to the free plan at the end of your 30-day trial.

Can Harvest create a purchase order before payment?
Yes, we can arrange a quote or purchase order for companies with 10+ people that require it. Contact us at sales@harvestapp.com with your request.

Can I pay for Harvest and Forecast together?
No, Harvest and Forecast (our time planning app) are currently billed separately. However, we do offer a 10% discount for using Harvest and Forecast together. You can reach out to us at sales@harvestapp.com for more information.
